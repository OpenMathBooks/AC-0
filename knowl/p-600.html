In this case where \(h(x) = \frac{x+1}{x^2+2x-1}\) the degree of the denominator is larger than the degree of the numerator so \(h(x) \to 0\) as \(x \to \infty\text{.}\) Therefore, there is a horizontal asymptote at \(y=0\text{.}\) Observe that \(h(-1) = 0\) so the function's graph does indeed cross the horizontal asymptote.
