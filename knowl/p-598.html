For \(f(x) = \frac{x+3}{5x-2}\) the degrees of the two polynomials are both \(1\) so the numerator and denominator grow at the same rate. The horizontal asymptote will be the ratio of the coefficients: \(f(x) \to \frac{1}{5}\) as \(x \to \infty\text{.}\) The graph does not cross the horizontal asymptote.
