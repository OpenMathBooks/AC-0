Item1

This function clearly has two roots and the ends show the same behavior. This indicates that this is likely a degree 2 polynomial (a quadratic). Based on the roots \(x=-2\) and \(x=1\) the factors are \((x+2)\) and \((x-1)\) so the polynomial is of the form \(a(x) = C(x+2)(x-1)\text{.}\) Finally, it appears that the point \((-1,-4)\) is on the curve so \(C=2\) and the polynomial is

\begin{equation*} a(x) = 2(x+2)(x-1). \end{equation*}
