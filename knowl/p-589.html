When considering the functions \(f(x) = x^3 + 2x^2 - x + 7\) and \(g(x) = x^2+4x+2\text{,}\) the cubic function, \(f(x)\text{,}\) will dominate as \(x \to \infty\text{.}\) Therefore, as \(x \to \infty\text{,}\) \(h(x) = f(x)/g(x)\) will go to infinity and \(k(x) = g(x)/f(x)\) will go to zero.
