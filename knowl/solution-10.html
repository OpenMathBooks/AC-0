Solution1.2.4.1

  1. \(P(t) = P_0 \cdot 2^{t/20}\) where \(t\) is measured in minutes.

  2. Assuming that \(P_0 = 1\text{,}\) \(P(1440) = 2^{1440/20} \approx 4.72 \times 10^{21}\text{.}\)

  3. \begin{align*} 6.0 \times 10^{27} \amp = 1.7 \times 10^{-12} \cdot 1 \cdot 2^{t/20}\\ 3.529 \times 10^{39} \amp = 2^{t/20}\\ \log_2\left( 3.529 \times 10^{39} \right) \amp = \frac{t}{20}\\ 20 \log_2\left( 3.529 \times 10^{39} \right) \amp = t\\ \amp \approx 2627.5 \text{ minutes } \end{align*}
