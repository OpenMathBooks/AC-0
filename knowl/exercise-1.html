  1. Using graph paper make a plot of the data on appropriate axes.

  2. Do the data appear linear? Why or why not?

  3. Pick two representative points from the data and use them to write the equation of a line that fits the data. Plot your line on top of your data and discuss how well your line fits the data. (This may take a few attempts.)

  4. Despite how well your data fit a linear model, it is not entirely sensible to use a linear model for this data. Why?
