Suppose \(f(x) = x^{3} + 2x^{2}-x + 7\) and \(g(x) = x^{2} + 4x + 2\text{.}\)

  1. Which function dominates as \(x \to \infty\text{?}\)

  2. What is the behavior of the function \(h(x) = \displaystyle{\frac {f(x)}{g(x)}}\) as \(x \to \infty\text{?}\)

  3. What is the behavior of the function \(k(x) = \displaystyle{\frac {g(x)}{f(x)}}\) as \(x \to \infty\text{?}\)
