Item1

Suppose \(f(x) = x^{2}+ 3x + 2\) and \(g(x) = x - 3\text{.}\)

  1. What is the behavior of the function \(h(x) = \displaystyle{\frac {f(x)}{g(x)}}\) near \(x = -1\text{?}\) (i.e. what happens to \(h(x)\) as \(x\to -1\text{?}\)) near \(x = -2\text{?}\) near \(x = 3\text{?}\)

  2. What is the behavior of the function \(k(x) = \displaystyle{\frac {g(x)}{f(x)}}\) near \(x = -1\text{?}\) near \(x = -2\text{?}\) near \(x = 3\text{?}\)
