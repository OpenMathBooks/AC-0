We need to solve the equation \(65+186e^{-0.06t} = 120\text{.}\) Subtracting 65 and dividing by 186 gives \(e^{-0.06t} = \frac{55}{186}\text{.}\) Taking the natural logarithm of both sides and then dividing by -0.06 gives \(t = -\frac{1}{0.06} \ln \left( \frac{55}{186} \right)\text{.}\)
