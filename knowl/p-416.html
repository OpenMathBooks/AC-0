A hot bowl of soup is served at a dinner party. It starts to cool according to Newton's Law of Cooling so its temperature, \(T\) (measured in degrees Fahrenheit) after \(t\) minutes is given by

\begin{equation*} T(t) = 65 + 186 e^{-0.06t}. \end{equation*}

How long will it take from the time the food is served until the temperature is \(120^\circ\)F?
