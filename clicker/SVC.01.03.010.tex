\item The functions $f$ and $g$ have values given in the table below.  What is the value of $f(g(0))$?

\begin{tabular}{|c|c|c|c|c|c|}
\hline
$x$ & -2 & -1 & 0 & 1 & 2 \\
\hline
$f(x)$ & 1 & 0 & -2 & 2 & -1 \\
\hline
$g(x)$ & -1 & 1 & 2 & 0 & -2 \\
\hline
\end{tabular}

\begin{enumerate}
\item -2 
\item -1 
\item 0 
\item 1 
\item 2 
\end{enumerate}
