\item The function $f(x)$ goes through the point $A$ with coordinates (2,3).  $g(x) = 2 f( \frac{1}{3} x - 2) + 4$.  What are the coordinates of point $A$ in the function $g(x)$?

\begin{enumerate}
\item $(4,10)$
\item $(4,-\frac{5}{2})$
\item $(12,10)$
\item $(-\frac{4}{3},10)$
\item $(-\frac{4}{3},-\frac{5}{2})$
\end{enumerate}
