\item
Caffeine leaves the body at a continuous rate of $17\%$ per hour.  How much caffeine is left in the body 8 hours after drinking a cup of coffee containing 100 mg of caffeine?

\begin{enumerate}
\item 389.62 mg
\item 22.52 mg
\item 25.67 mg
\item There is no way to tell.
\end{enumerate}
