\item What is $\displaystyle 3^{-1}=\frac{1}{3}$ in logarithmic form?

\begin{enumerate}
\item $\displaystyle \log_3 (-1)=\frac{1}{3}$
\item $\displaystyle \log_{-1} \frac{1}{3}=3$
\item $\displaystyle \log_{\frac{1}{3}} 3=-1$
\item $\displaystyle \log_3 \frac{1}{3}=-1$
\end{enumerate}
