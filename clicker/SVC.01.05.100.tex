\item Which of the basic trig functions below are odd functions?

\begin{enumerate}
\item $f(x) = \sin(x)$.
\item $f(x) = \cos(x)$.
\item $f(x) = \tan(x)$.
\item (a) and (b).
\item (a) and (c).
\item (b) and (c).
\item (a), (b), and (c).
\item None of the above.
\end{enumerate}
